## 1. Strategy Decision

Optimaks must not become a generic social media agency or video production company.

Short video content is allowed only when it supports the Optimaks workflow positioning:

```text
Short Video Content
↓
Landing Page
↓
WhatsApp CTA
↓
Lead Capture
↓
Quote / Booking / Job / Invoice Workflow
```

The correct offer name is:

```text
Short Video Lead Kit
```

This is an add-on to Optimaks Service OS / Aircon Template, not a standalone social media management product.

## 3. Strategic Rationale

Optimaks workflow systems need enough leads entering the funnel before quote, booking, job card, invoice, and reminder modules can show value.

Short video supports the top of funnel, but the Optimaks value is not video production. The Optimaks value is the conversion bridge from video traffic to tracked workflow.

Short video is especially suitable for aircon and field service because these industries have visual proof assets:

```text
- dirty filter before / after
- aircon water leakage
- chemical wash process
- technician inspection
- HDB / condo on-site repair
- service explanation
- completed job proof
```

---

## 4. Competitive Boundary

Optimaks should not compete as:

```text
- social media management agency
- TikTok posting agency
- high-end corporate video production house
- influencer agency
- performance ads agency
```

Optimaks should compete as:

```text
Website-to-Workflow system provider with optional short-video-powered lead intake.
```

---

## 5. Recommended Vendor Strategy

Use external vendors for filming and editing execution.

Optimaks keeps control over:

```text
- customer workflow diagnosis
- monthly content theme
- hook and script direction
- WhatsApp CTA
- landing page mapping
- UTM / lead source tracking
- reporting and conversion review
```

Vendors provide:

```text
- filming
- editing
- subtitles
- audio / pacing
- vertical output for TikTok / Reels / Shorts
- basic revisions within limits
```

---

## 6. Priority Vendor Types

Prioritize:

1. JB / Malaysia short video execution teams.
2. White-label content production vendors.
3. UGC / micro-creator content agencies.
4. Small Singapore content studios for local backup.
5. Vendors comfortable with service business footage and recurring monthly delivery.

Avoid:

1. High-end cinematic corporate video agencies.
2. Branding-only agencies that do not understand lead conversion.
3. Agencies requiring large monthly ad budgets.
4. Vendors unwilling to work white-label or hybrid.
5. Vendors who insist on client ownership or watermarking.

| Priority | Vendor Type | Strategic Use |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | JB short video execution team | Low-cost filming / editing for Growth package |
| 2 | White-label digital / UGC agency | Partner delivery and UGC on-site experience |
| 3 | UGC content factory | Starter package editing and high-volume content output |
| 4 | Singapore white-label agency | Local backup and client-facing support when needed |
| 5 | Flexible point / seasonal vendor | Campaign bursts during peak seasons |

## 8. Recommended Commercial Direction

Do not sell short video as an isolated product.

The best early-stage bundle is:

```text
Optimaks Growth Package
+
Short Video Starter or Short Video Growth Add-on
```

Example:

```text
S$2,800 setup + S$199/month
+
S$499/month Short Video Starter
```

or

```text
S$2,800 setup + S$199/month
+
S$1,500/month Short Video Growth
```

---

## 9. Strategic Guardrails

1. No guarantee of views, followers, leads, sales, or revenue.
2. No unlimited revisions.
3. No daily social media operation unless separately scoped.
4. No paid ads management unless a qualified partner is explicitly assigned.
5. No vendor direct-selling to Optimaks clients.
6. No filming without client-provided site access and consent.
7. Short video claims must match approved sales and legal documents.
