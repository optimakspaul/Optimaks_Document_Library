## 1. Core Positioning

Optimaks should not position itself as another generic web agency or a heavy CRM / ERP vendor.

Optimaks should position itself as:

```text
A lightweight website-to-workflow system provider for Singapore service SMEs.
```

Full positioning statement:

```text
Optimaks helps Singapore aircon and service SMEs turn online enquiries into scheduled jobs, completed work, and paid invoices through a lightweight website, WhatsApp lead capture, and workflow automation system — without the cost and complexity of heavy CRM or ERP.
```

Chinese version:

```text
Optimaks 幫新加坡冷氣與服務型 SME，打造從網站詢問、WhatsApp 接單、報價、預約、派工、完工到收款追蹤的一體化輕量系統，不需要導入昂貴又笨重的 CRM 或 ERP。
```

---

## 2. Strategic Market Gap

The Singapore SME digital market often separates into two sides:

```text
Web / marketing agencies = front-end presence and traffic.
FSM / CRM / ERP vendors = backend operations and job management.
```

Optimaks' opportunity is the bridge:

```text
Website / Landing Page
↓
WhatsApp Lead Capture
↓
Quote / Booking / Job Workflow
↓
Invoice / Maintenance Follow-up
```

Sales phrase:

```text
Web agencies bring you enquiries.
FSM software helps you manage jobs.
Optimaks connects both — so every enquiry has a clear path to quotation, booking, job completion, and payment.
```

---

## 3. Primary Target Customer

Initial target:

```text
Singapore aircon and field service SMEs with 3-10 workers or technicians.
```

Typical signs:

```text
- receives enquiries through WhatsApp, Google Maps, referral, or basic website
- has no clear lead tracking system
- uses WhatsApp / Excel / memory to manage jobs
- has repeated service, maintenance, or follow-up opportunities
- not ready for heavy ERP / CRM implementation
```

---

## 4. Not Target Customer, Initially

Optimaks should not initially target:

```text
- large enterprises requiring full ERP
- companies needing complex finance / inventory integration from day one
- clients whose only priority is lowest-cost website design
- companies that only want ad management without workflow change
- clients demanding full custom software without productized boundaries
```

---

## 5. Primary Message

```text
You do not just need another website.
You need a system that turns enquiries into completed jobs.
```

Chinese:

```text

你需要一套把詢問變成成交、派工、完工和收款的系統。
```

---

## 6. Strategic Rule

Externally, Optimaks should look focused.

Internally, Optimaks should remain reusable.

```text
External positioning = Aircon / Field Service focus.
Internal architecture = Service OS core + industry templates.
```
