## 1. GTM Objective

The first 30 days should validate whether Singapore aircon / field service SMEs respond to the website-to-workflow message.

Primary goal:

```text
Secure first paid pilot / founding client.
```

Secondary goals:

```text
- validate pitch
- build prospect list
- complete demo flow
- collect objections
- refine package pricing
```

---

## 2. Week 1 — Positioning and Demo

Deliverables:

```text
- final positioning statement
- 3 pricing packages
- landing page wireframe
- aircon demo flow
- outreach script
```

Demo flow:

```text
Enquiry → Quote → Booking → Job Card → Invoice Status → Maintenance Reminder
```

---

## 3. Week 2 — Prospect List and Personalization

Build a list of 100 prospects.

Selection criteria:

```text
- Singapore aircon service company
- Google Maps listing
- 4.0+ rating preferred
- website is weak or lacks clear booking CTA
- WhatsApp / phone is main contact channel
- appears to have more than one worker or service team
```

Track:

```text
- company name
- website
- Google rating
- WhatsApp / phone
- observed problem
- pitch angle
- outreach status
```

---

## 4. Week 3 — Outreach and Discovery

Target activity:

```text
50 messages sent
10 replies
5 discovery calls
2 demo meetings
1 paid diagnostic or pilot
```

Primary offer:

```text
Free Workflow Review
```

Alternative paid entry:

```text
S$800 Workflow Diagnostic, offset against setup fee
```

---

## 5. Week 4 — Pilot and Case Study

Target outcome:

```text
1 founding client or paid pilot
```

Founding offer:

```text
S$1,999 setup + S$149/month
Limited to first 3 clients
```

Pilot deliverables:

```text
- mini landing page
- WhatsApp CTA
- lead dashboard
- quote / booking tracker
- before / after workflow report
```

Case study evidence:

```text
- enquiry capture before / after
- admin time saved
- quote follow-up visibility
- booking clarity
- client testimonial, if available
```
