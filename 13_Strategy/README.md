# 13 Strategy

Current Optimaks strategy documents for `v1.5.5`.

This folder is the official location for approved market positioning, competitor review, pricing packages, GTM planning, SWOT, Porter Five Forces, Lanchester strategy, and AI market research strategy review workflow.

## Important Rule

Research Digest is not strategy. Strategy becomes official only when reviewed and stored in this folder.
