## 1. Strategic Principle

Optimaks should use a weak-player Lanchester strategy.

This means:

```text
Do not fight large competitors across a broad market.
Choose a small battlefield.
Concentrate message, product, outreach and proof.
Become locally / segment-wise recognizable before expanding.
```

---

## 2. Battlefields to Avoid

Optimaks should not initially fight these broad battlefields:

```text
- all SME digital transformation
- all website design
- all CRM
- all ERP
- all automation projects
- all social media marketing
- all PSG software
- all service industries at once
```

These battlefields are too broad and filled with stronger or more established competitors.

---

## 3. First Beachhead

The first focused battlefield is:

```text
Singapore aircon and field service SMEs with 3-10 workers or technicians who rely on WhatsApp, Google Maps, Excel and manual admin to manage enquiries and jobs.
```

Why this beachhead is suitable:

| Reason | Strategic Value |
|---|---|
| Clear workflow pain | Enquiry, quote, booking, job card and invoice tracking are easy to explain. |
| WhatsApp-first behavior | Fits local SME communication habits. |
| Repeat service | Maintenance reminders and follow-up create recurring value. |
| Template potential | Aircon workflows can become reusable templates. |
| Prospect visibility | Google Maps and websites make prospecting practical. |
| Not too enterprise-heavy | Smaller teams need lighter alternatives to ERP / CRM. |

---

## 4. First Offer

Primary offer:

```text
Aircon Website-to-Workflow System
```

Core promise:

```text
Stop losing jobs in WhatsApp.
Turn enquiries into tracked quotes, bookings, completed jobs and invoice status.
```

Main package:

```text
Growth Package — Lead-to-Booking Workflow
```

Support offers:

```text
Starter = Lead Capture Website
Pro = Service Workflow OS
Short Video Lead Kit = Traffic-to-Lead support add-on
Diagnostic = Sales entry offer
```

---

## 5. Lanchester Stage Plan

### Stage 1 — Single Segment Proof

Goal:

```text
Win 1-3 founding aircon / field service clients.
```

Required evidence:

```text
- before / after workflow
- demo screenshots
- lead capture example
- quote / booking tracking example
- job card example
- testimonial or owner feedback
```

---

### Stage 2 — Local Segment Recognition

Goal:

```text
Become known as the practical Website-to-Workflow system for small Singapore aircon / field service teams.
```

Actions:

```text
- publish aircon-specific landing page
- run Google Maps prospecting
- use cold outreach and workflow review
- create aircon demo flow
- maintain clear package pricing
```

---

### Stage 3 — Template Replication

After Aircon OS is proven, expand to adjacent service industries:

```text
Aircon
↓
Cleaning
↓
Pest Control
↓
Plumbing
↓
Electrical
↓
Appliance Repair
↓
Facility Maintenance
```

Core workflow remains:

```text
Lead → Customer → Quote → Booking → Job Card → Invoice Status → Reminder
```

Industry differences should be handled through:

```text
- copy
- service types
- job card fields
- reminder logic
- report labels
- landing page examples
```

---

## 6. Outreach Target Rule

Initial outreach should prioritize companies with:

```text
- Singapore location
- aircon / HVAC / field service category
- Google Review rating around 4.0+
- visible WhatsApp / phone contact
- weak or basic website
- no strong booking / workflow system visible
- signs of active operations
```

Avoid initial prospects that:

```text
- already use complex enterprise systems
- only want the cheapest possible website
- demand guaranteed leads without tracking and sales process changes
- need full ERP / accounting / HR / payroll immediately
```

---

## 7. Sales Message Rule

Primary message:

```text
You do not need another website.
You need a system that turns enquiries into completed jobs.
```

Aircon-focused message:

```text
From WhatsApp enquiry to completed aircon job — one simple workflow.
```

Anti-marketplace message:

```text
Build your own customer database instead of depending only on platforms.
```

Anti-ERP message:

```text
Too small for ERP. Too busy for Excel.
Optimaks is the practical middle step.
```

---

## 8. Product Rule

Externally:

```text
Sound focused on aircon / field service.
```

Internally:

```text
Build reusable Service OS modules.
```

Do not hard-code features only for aircon when the same pattern can serve other service industries.
