## 1. Why This Is v1.5.4

This release is not merely a small correction to v1.5.3.

v1.5.3 defined how Optimaks should use Gemini or other large-context tools for research intake and multi-model handoff.

v1.5.4 adds a new governance layer that turns market research into official business decisions:

```text
Market Research
↓
Strategy Review
↓
Market Positioning
↓
Pricing
↓
GTM Plan
↓
Sales Playbook
↓
Product Strategy
```

Because this affects business positioning, pricing, product packaging and sales execution, it must be treated as its own version.

---

## 2. What v1.5.4 Adds

v1.5.4 formally governs:

1. AI market research digest handling.
2. Competitor research review.
3. ChatGPT strategy review.
4. Optimaks market positioning.
5. Pricing package structure.
6. GTM planning.
7. Sales scripts and discovery questions.
8. Product strategy documents for Optimaks Service OS and Aircon Template.
9. Rules for converting research into GitHub-ready product scope.

---

## 3. What v1.5.4 Does Not Do

v1.5.4 does not replace:

- v1.5.2 GitHub / AI code execution governance.
- v1.5.3 AI Research Intake / Multi-Model Handoff governance.
- Existing QA, PDPA, ISO-ready, grant-ready, and delivery standards.

v1.5.4 also does not mean market research can directly become code.

---

## 4. Governance Rule

```text
Research Digest is supporting material.
Strategy Review is decision preparation.
Approved strategy docs are the Source of Truth.
GitHub Issues are the implementation trigger.
```
