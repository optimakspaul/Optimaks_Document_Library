## 1. Scope Decision

v1.5.7 extends the Optimaks Constitution by adding a controlled partner-governed short video lead generation add-on.

This release converts the approved short video vendor research and strategy review into official Optimaks documents.

---

## 2. Approved Direction

Optimaks may offer short video support only as a workflow-connected lead generation add-on.

Approved positioning:

```text
Optimaks is not a social media agency.
Optimaks helps service SMEs turn content traffic into tracked WhatsApp enquiries, quotes, bookings, jobs and payment follow-ups.
```

---

## 3. Out of Scope

v1.5.7 does not approve Optimaks to become:

```text
- social media management agency
- video production house
- paid ads management agency
- influencer agency
- guarantee-based lead provider
```

## 5. Governance Rule

External video partners are execution partners only.

They do not control:

```text
- Optimaks strategy
- package pricing
- client ownership
- CTA design
- workflow design
- lead tracking logic
- final quality acceptance
```

Founder review remains the final quality gate.
