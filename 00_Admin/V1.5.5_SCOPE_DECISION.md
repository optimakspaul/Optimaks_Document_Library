## 1. Decision

v1.5.5 is approved as the active strategic constitution guardrail patch for Optimaks.

It adds SWOT, Porter Five Forces, Lanchester strategy and constitution-level decision rules to the v1.5.4 AI Market Research and Strategy Governance release.

---

## 2. Why This Patch Is Needed

v1.5.4 defined market research, positioning, pricing, GTM and sales governance.

However, Optimaks also needs a clear rule for how those strategy outputs should control future decisions.

v1.5.5 answers:

```text
What should go into the constitution?
What should remain strategy support?
What should be rejected as scope drift?
How should AI-generated ideas be judged before implementation?
```

---

## 3. Approved Strategic Guardrails

Optimaks should remain:

```text
A lightweight Website-to-Workflow system provider for Singapore service SMEs.
```

Optimaks should not drift into:

```text
- generic web agency
- pure social media management agency
- heavy ERP / CRM vendor
- PSG grant reseller
- unfocused custom software shop
```

First beachhead:

```text
Singapore aircon / field service SMEs with 3-10 workers or technicians.
```

Core workflow:

```text
Enquiry → Lead → Quote → Booking → Job Card → Invoice Status → Reminder
```
