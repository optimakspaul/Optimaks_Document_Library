---

## 1. Product Definition

Short Video Lead Kit is an add-on module for Optimaks Service OS / Aircon Template.

It helps service SMEs convert short video traffic into tracked enquiries and workflow actions.

```text
Video View
↓
Landing Page / Profile Link
↓
WhatsApp CTA
↓
Lead Capture
↓
Quote / Booking / Job / Invoice Tracking
```

It is not a standalone social media management service.

---

## 2. Product Goals

The add-on exists to:

1. Create more lead entry points for service SMEs.
2. Provide content themes that connect to real service pain points.
3. Make every short video point to a clear WhatsApp / landing page CTA.
4. Track lead source and enquiry status inside Optimaks workflow.
5. Increase the perceived monthly value of the Optimaks system.

---

## 3. Package A — Short Video Starter

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Suggested Price | SGD 399–599 / month |
| Best For | Small service teams willing to film mobile footage themselves |
| Delivery Model | Client records footage; vendor edits; Optimaks provides strategy and CTA |

### Included

```text
- 4 short videos per month
- 4 monthly content themes
- hook and caption direction
- WhatsApp CTA wording
- mobile shooting checklist
- lead source tracking setup
```

### Optimaks Responsibilities

```text
- monthly content theme planning
- hook / script direction
- CTA design
- landing page / WhatsApp mapping
- lead tracking setup
```

### Vendor Responsibilities

```text
- edit client-provided footage
- add subtitles
- optimize pacing
- output vertical video format
- one minor revision per video
```

### Client Responsibilities

```text
- record sufficient footage each month
- upload footage by the agreed date
- capture before / after and technician process clips
- approve minor edits within agreed timeline
```

### Exclusions

```text
- no on-site videographer
- no paid ads
- no daily social posting
- no comment management
- no guaranteed views or leads
```

```text
- hook / script direction

- performance review
```

### Vendor Responsibilities

```text
- on-site filming
- technician / service process capture
- editing and subtitles
- vertical video output
- one minor revision per video
```


## 6. System Requirements

The Optimaks system should support:

```text
- lead_source field
- campaign_source field
- video_id / content_theme field
- UTM tracking
- WhatsApp CTA link tracking
- enquiry status
- quote status
- booking status
- job completion status
- invoice status
```

---

## 7. MVP Boundary

For MVP, implement only:

```text
- Short Video Starter package definition
- lead source tracking
- WhatsApp CTA mapping
- simple monthly content calendar
- client shooting checklist
```

Defer:

```text
- UGC creator coordination
- automated ad reporting
- multi-platform analytics integration
- AI content performance prediction
```

---

## 8. Acceptance Criteria

A Short Video Lead Kit delivery is acceptable only when:

```text
- video output follows approved hook / CTA
- WhatsApp or landing page CTA is clearly visible
- lead source tracking is configured
- client responsibilities are documented
- revision count is within scope
- no unapproved claims are made
```
