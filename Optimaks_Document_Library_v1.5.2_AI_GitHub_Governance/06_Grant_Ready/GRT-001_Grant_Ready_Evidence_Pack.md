## Purpose

Grant-ready does not mean applying immediately. It means keeping evidence that shows project purpose, cost, capability improvement, productivity gain, and business outcome.

## Evidence Pack

1. Company positioning
2. Project objective
3. Current pain point
4. Solution blueprint
5. Budget / quotation
6. Implementation timeline
7. Screenshots / preview links
8. Before/after workflow comparison
9. Productivity or revenue impact
10. Case study and outcome report
