## 1. Prospect Criteria

Prioritize companies with:

```text
- Singapore-based aircon / field service business
- Google Maps listing
- 4.0+ rating preferred
- weak website or no clear booking CTA
- WhatsApp / phone as primary enquiry channel
- visible service team or repeat-service model
```

---

## 2. Email Script

Subject:

```text
Quick idea to help {{Company Name}} capture and track more aircon enquiries
```

Body:

```text
Hi {{Owner Name}},

I came across {{Company Name}} while looking at aircon service providers in Singapore.

Many aircon companies already receive enquiries through WhatsApp, Google Maps, or their website — but the hard part is usually what happens after that:

enquiries get buried, quotes are not followed up, bookings are tracked manually, and completed jobs are not always linked clearly to invoice or maintenance follow-up.

I run Optimaks, and I help aircon and service companies build a lightweight website-to-workflow system.

It can include:

- a mobile-friendly landing page
- WhatsApp lead capture
- enquiry dashboard
- quote and booking tracking
- job completion record
- invoice status tracking
- maintenance reminder flow

The goal is simple: help every enquiry move clearly toward a scheduled and completed job.

This is not a heavy ERP or complicated CRM. It is designed for small service teams that want something practical and fast to launch.

Would you be open to a quick 15-minute workflow review? I can show you where enquiries may be leaking and what a simple system could look like for {{Company Name}}.

Best,
Paul
Optimaks Pte Ltd
```

## 4. Follow-Up Sequence

```text
Day 0: Personalized first message
Day 3: Follow-up with one observed website / WhatsApp issue
Day 7: Offer free workflow review
Day 14: Send sample aircon demo screenshot / flow
Day 30: Final light-touch follow-up
```

---

## 5. Objection Handling

| Objection | Response Direction |
|---|---|
| We already have a website. | The question is what happens after the enquiry. Optimaks helps track quote, booking, job and invoice flow. |
| We use WhatsApp already. | Optimaks does not replace WhatsApp; it adds structure around WhatsApp enquiries so leads are not lost. |
| We do not want ERP. | Optimaks is intentionally lighter than ERP. It starts with the 3-5 workflows you need now. |
| We want PSG. | PSG can be useful, but Optimaks is for businesses that want a faster, lighter launch without long paperwork. |
| We are too small. | Small teams often lose more time because everything depends on owner memory and WhatsApp. Start with Starter or Growth. |
```
