---

## 2. Client Problem Statement

```text
Many aircon and service companies can post videos, but the enquiries still disappear into WhatsApp chats.
Optimaks connects content traffic to a clear service workflow.
```

---

## 3. Discovery Questions

Ask:

```text
1. Do you currently post short videos or service photos?
2. Which platform gets the most attention: Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts?
3. When someone watches your content, where do they go next?
4. Do you know which video led to which WhatsApp enquiry?
5. Do you track whether that enquiry became a quote, booking, job or invoice?
6. Who records job-site photos or videos today?
7. Are your technicians comfortable filming short clips?
8. Would you prefer self-shot content or monthly on-site filming?
```

---

## 4. Offer Explanation

```text
Our Short Video Lead Kit is not normal social media management.

We help plan service-based short videos, connect each video to a WhatsApp or landing page CTA, and track whether enquiries become quotes, bookings and completed jobs.

The filming and editing can be handled by our execution partner, while Optimaks handles the conversion workflow.
```

---

## 5. Package Pitch

### Starter

```text
Best if your team can record simple mobile footage during real jobs.
We provide the monthly topics, hooks, shooting checklist, CTA, and lead tracking. Our editing partner turns your footage into 4 short videos.
```

### Growth

```text
Best if you want professional on-site service demo content.
A video partner comes once a month to film real work. Optimaks plans the scripts and connects each video to WhatsApp and your workflow dashboard.
```

### Pro

```text
Best if you want UGC-style customer experience videos and detailed campaign tracking.
This is for teams ready to treat short video as a full lead generation channel.
```

---

## 6. Objection Handling

### “Can you guarantee leads?”

```text
We do not guarantee a fixed number of leads because views and enquiries depend on many factors: offer, timing, platform, audience and response speed.

What we do guarantee is that each video will have a clear CTA and tracking path, so you can see which content is bringing enquiries into your workflow.
```

### “Why not just hire a video agency?”

```text
A video agency can help create content.
Optimaks helps connect that content to your service workflow — WhatsApp enquiry, quote, booking, job completion and invoice follow-up.
```

### “Can we start small?”

```text
Yes. Start with Short Video Starter: your team records simple mobile footage, and we turn it into trackable short videos linked to your WhatsApp enquiry flow.
```
