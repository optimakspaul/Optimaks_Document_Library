---

## 1. Enquiry Source

```text
1. Where do most new enquiries come from today?
2. Website, Google Maps, referral, Facebook, WhatsApp, platform, or repeat customers?
3. How many enquiries do you receive per week?
4. How many are missed or replied to late?
```

---

## 2. WhatsApp and Follow-Up

```text
1. Who replies to WhatsApp enquiries?
2. Do you label or track enquiry status?
3. How do you know whether a quote was followed up?
4. Do old enquiries get reviewed again?
```

---

## 3. Quote and Booking

```text
1. How do you create quotes today?
2. Are quotes stored in WhatsApp, Excel, PDF, or accounting software?
3. How do you schedule bookings?
4. How do technicians know their assigned jobs?
```

---

## 4. Job Completion

```text
1. How do technicians report completed jobs?
2. Do they send photos?
3. Who checks completion before invoice?
4. Are customer complaints linked to previous job records?
```

---

## 5. Invoice and Reminder

```text
1. How do you track whether invoice has been sent or paid?
2. Do you remind customers for maintenance after 3-6 months?
3. Is repeat servicing currently tracked manually?
```

---

## 6. Qualification

```text
1. What would make this system worth paying for?
2. Which problem is most painful: more leads, less admin, faster quote, job tracking, or payment follow-up?
3. Who must approve this purchase?
4. Do you prefer setup fee + monthly fee, or a smaller diagnostic first?
```
