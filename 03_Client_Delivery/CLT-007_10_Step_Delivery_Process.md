## Ten Steps

1. **Discovery** — Understand client industry, pain points, tools, budget, decision maker, and success standard.
2. **Scope Definition** — Define project scope, exclusions, deliverables, and payment milestones.
3. **Solution Blueprint** — Design modules, data flow, user roles, UI flow, and integration method.
4. **Proposal / Quotation** — Produce formal pricing, setup fee, monthly fee, maintenance scope, and deliverables.
5. **Contract / E-Sign** — Complete agreement through tools such as Zoho Sign or Signwell.
6. **Implementation** — Use GitHub Issue + Branch + AI tools for development.
7. **Staging / Preview** — Deploy test version first; do not directly replace production.
8. **QA & Acceptance** — Complete mobile, desktop, data, permission, and workflow tests.
9. **Production Deployment** — Merge and deploy only after acceptance.
10. **Handover & Maintenance** — Provide user guide, account/access notes, maintenance report, and monthly service.

This process also applies to Optimaks OS internal development, with the client role replaced by internal user or future customer needs.
