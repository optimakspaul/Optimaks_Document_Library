## 1. Core Company Positioning

Optimaks is an AI-assisted SME workflow system builder based in Singapore.

Optimaks does not position itself as a generic website vendor, generic CRM reseller, or generic SaaS product company.

Optimaks builds industry-specific Workflow Automation Systems and SME Operating OS solutions that help business owners systemize real daily operations.

中文定位：

> Optimaks 不做通用 SaaS。我們為特定產業打造可落地的營運 OS，從接單、報價、排程、施工、收款到客戶回流，協助 SME 把日常流程系統化。

---

## 2. Primary Direction

Optimaks develops reusable digital operating systems for SMEs, beginning with:

1. Internal CRM / workflow automation tools.
2. Optimaks OS as the internal operating backbone.
3. Aircon OS as the first vertical productized workflow system.
4. ACRA Radar as an internal prospecting and lead research tool.
5. Client workflow automation systems for selected SME industries.

The goal is not to build disconnected tools. The goal is to build repeatable operating systems around industry workflows.

---

## 3. Product Positioning Principle

Each Optimaks system should be designed around a real business workflow, not around an abstract feature list.

A valid Optimaks product should usually answer:

1. What industry is this for?
2. What daily workflow does it systemize?
3. What manual coordination does it reduce?
4. What business status does it make visible?
5. What monthly or recurring value can be shown to the client?
6. What Small Core MVP can be built first?
7. What modules can be added later without breaking the core?

---

## 4. Applicable Projects

This development constitution applies to:

1. **Optimaks OS** — internal CRM, workflow automation, proposal, contract, maintenance report, and client portal.
2. **Aircon OS** — verticalized SME operating system for aircon service companies.
3. **ACRA Radar** — internal prospecting and lead research tool.
4. **Client Workflow Automation Projects** — landing pages, lead capture, CRM, booking, quote, job status, invoice tracking, reports, and maintenance follow-up.
5. **MVP / Prototype Projects** — quick validation tools before formal integration.
6. **Internal Tools** — reporting, quote generation, maintenance report, prospecting, automation, and operations support.

---

## 5. What Optimaks Does Not Prioritize

Optimaks should avoid positioning itself as:

1. A generic SaaS platform for everyone.
2. A pure website design vendor.
3. A pure CRM implementation vendor.
4. A custom software shop that rebuilds everything from zero for every client.
5. A system that promises to fully replace all human workers.

Optimaks may automate repetitive admin coordination, but the purpose is to support SME owners and staff, not to claim full human replacement.

---

## 6. Operating Style Principle

Optimaks should look and operate like a professional systemized company even when starting as a one-person company.

The system must be:

- repeatable
- traceable
- reviewable
- client-deliverable
- AI-ready
- ISO-ready in mindset
- grant-ready in evidence collection
- industry-workflow-first
- Small Core First
