Optimaks is positioned as an AI-assisted SME digital system builder based in Singapore, starting from internal tools and productized SME vertical systems.

## Primary Direction

Optimaks develops reusable digital operating systems for SMEs, beginning with internal CRM / automation tools and the first vertical product, Aircon OS.

## Applicable Projects

This development constitution applies to:

1. **Optimaks OS** — internal CRM, automation, proposal, contract, maintenance report, and client portal.
2. **Aircon OS** — verticalized SME suite for aircon service companies.
3. **ACRA Radar** — internal prospecting and lead research tool.
4. **Client Projects** — landing pages, CRMs, automations, dashboards, booking, invoice, reports.
5. **MVP / Prototype Projects** — quick validation tools before formal integration.
6. **Internal Tools** — reporting, quote generation, maintenance report, prospecting, automation.

## Positioning Principle

Optimaks should look and operate like a professional systemized company even when starting as a one-person company. The system must be:

- repeatable
- traceable
- reviewable
- client-deliverable
- AI-ready
- ISO-ready in mindset
- grant-ready in evidence collection
