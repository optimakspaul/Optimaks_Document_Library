---

## 1. Important Notice

This document is an internal commercial checklist, not legal advice.

Formal agreements should be reviewed by a qualified legal professional before client or vendor signature.

---

## 2. Required Vendor Terms

Every video vendor agreement should cover:

```text
- scope of work
- deliverables
- pricing
- turnaround time
- revision limits
- cancellation / reschedule rules
- no watermark requirement
- white-label / hybrid permission
- client non-solicitation
- confidentiality
- asset ownership / usage rights
- personal data handling
- filming consent responsibilities
- termination rights
```

---

## 3. Non-Solicitation Rule

Vendor must not directly solicit or sell to Optimaks clients introduced or managed by Optimaks.

Suggested clause:

```text
The vendor shall not directly approach, solicit, upsell, or enter into a separate commercial relationship with any client introduced by Optimaks, unless Optimaks provides written consent.
```

---

## 4. White-label / Brand Rule

When acting as an Optimaks execution partner, vendor must not add its own watermark, brand, logo, or sales contact to deliverables unless approved.

Suggested clause:

```text
All deliverables produced under white-label or hybrid engagement must be delivered without vendor watermark, logo, or direct sales contact information unless otherwise agreed in writing.
```

---

## 5. Revision Control

Suggested client-facing rule:

```text
Each video includes one minor revision. Additional revisions, major restructuring, re-editing, re-filming, or new creative direction are chargeable separately.
```

---

## 6. Filming Consent

Client must ensure that filming location, homeowner / tenant / staff permissions, and safety conditions are ready.

Suggested clause:

```text
The client is responsible for obtaining all necessary permissions for filming, including site access, homeowner or tenant consent, staff consent, and permission to use filmed materials for marketing purposes.
```

---

## 7. No Performance Guarantee

Suggested clause:

```text
Optimaks and its vendors provide content strategy, filming/editing support, CTA design, and workflow tracking. No specific views, followers, enquiries, leads, sales, rankings, or revenue outcomes are guaranteed.
```

---

## 8. Client Material Delay

For Starter packages:

```text
If the client does not provide required footage by the agreed deadline, the monthly fee remains payable. Optimaks may complete partial delivery based on available materials or roll planning forward, but no refund is due for client-caused material delays.
```

---

## 9. Vendor Data Handling

Vendors must not receive unnecessary sensitive data.

Avoid sharing:

```text
- client payment information
- full customer databases
- API keys
- Supabase credentials
- private customer addresses unless required for filming
- unapproved personal data
```

Use only:

```text
- filming brief
- shot list
- service scenario
- approved customer-facing contact details
- approved footage
```

---

## 10. Termination Triggers

Vendor may be removed from approved partner list if:

```text
- missed deadlines repeatedly
- directly solicits Optimaks clients
- adds unauthorized watermark
- exceeds agreed pricing without notice
- mishandles personal data
- delivers content that violates client brand or legal rules
- refuses to follow Optimaks CTA / workflow brief
```
