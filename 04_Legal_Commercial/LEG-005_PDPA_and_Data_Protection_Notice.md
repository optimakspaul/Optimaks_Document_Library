## Basic Notice Contents

A client-facing website or system should explain:

1. What personal data is collected.
2. Why it is collected.
3. How it is used.
4. Who can access it.
5. Whether third-party services are used.
6. How users can request correction/deletion when applicable.
7. Contact method for privacy questions.

## Internal Handling Rules

1. Collect only necessary data.
2. Keep credentials secure.
3. Use role-based access.
4. Avoid public exposure of sensitive data.
5. Export data for client handover when service ends, subject to contract.
