## Payment Terms Principles

1. Setup fee should be tied to project milestones.
2. Monthly fee should be clearly separated from setup fee.
3. Trial or pilot should not be confused with full official deployment.
4. Refund or guarantee terms must be written clearly if offered.
5. Add-ons require additional quote or Change Request approval.

## Suggested Terms

- Deposit before kickoff.
- Preview milestone before final deployment.
- Final payment before production or handover.
- Monthly fee begins after production launch or agreed date.
