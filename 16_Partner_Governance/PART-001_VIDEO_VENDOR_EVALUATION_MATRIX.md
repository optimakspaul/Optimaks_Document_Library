## 1. Vendor Evaluation Rule

A vendor may support Optimaks Short Video Lead Kit only if it can work under Optimaks strategy, CTA, workflow, and client ownership rules.

The vendor must not become the strategy owner or client owner.

---

## 2. Evaluation Criteria

| Criteria | Weight | Notes |
|---|---:|---|
| SME affordability | 15% | Must fit service SME budgets |
| Short video capability | 15% | TikTok / Reels / Shorts format required |
| Service business fit | 15% | Must handle aircon / repair / home service content |
| White-label / hybrid readiness | 15% | Must be willing to work behind Optimaks when needed |
| Recurring monthly delivery | 10% | Must handle 4–8 videos monthly |
| On-site filming ability | 10% | Important for Growth package |
| Editing from mobile footage | 10% | Important for Starter package |
| Turnaround time | 5% | Must be practical for monthly GTM cadence |
| Revision policy clarity | 5% | Must prevent revision creep |

## 4. Top Priority Vendor Profiles

### Profile A — JB Short Video Execution Partner

Best for:

```text
Short Video Growth
monthly filming
aircon before / after
service demo
```

Must support:

```text
- 1 on-site filming session per month
- 4–8 short video outputs
- white-label or hybrid delivery
- no watermark
- fixed revision limit
```

---

### Profile B — White-label / UGC Partner

Best for:

```text
Content-to-Workflow Pro
UGC customer experience content
creator-based service proof
```

Must support:

```text
- creator coordination
- on-site service experience shoot
- asset usage rights
- clear cancellation / no-show rules
```

---

### Profile C — Editing Factory

Best for:

```text
Short Video Starter
client mobile footage editing
monthly low-cost recurring output
```

Must support:

```text
- mobile footage cleanup
- subtitles
- vertical video output
- 4 videos/month recurring delivery
```

---

## 5. Vendor Questions

Ask every vendor:

```text
1. Do you accept white-label or hybrid partnership?
2. Can you produce 4–8 short videos per month for service businesses?
3. Can you follow scripts and hooks provided by Optimaks?
4. Can you shoot onsite for aircon / home service jobs?
5. Can you edit from customer-provided mobile footage?
6. What is your turnaround time?
7. How many revisions are included?
8. Can videos be delivered without your watermark?
9. Can you avoid direct sales contact with our clients?
10. Can you provide partner pricing for recurring monthly work?
11. Can you support English / Chinese subtitles?
12. Can you provide TikTok / Reels / Shorts format?
13. Can you sign non-solicitation / client ownership terms?
```

## 7. Approval Rule

A vendor may be used in a client-facing package only after:

```text
- qualification questions completed
- sample test completed or reviewed
- pricing confirmed
- revision policy confirmed
- no-watermark rule confirmed
- client non-solicitation rule accepted
- delivery checklist approved
```
