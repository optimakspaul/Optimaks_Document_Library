## 1. Email Template

**Subject:** Partnership enquiry for short video execution support

```text
Hi {{Name}},

I’m Paul from Optimaks Pte Ltd, based in Singapore.

We build lightweight workflow systems for service SMEs such as aircon, repair, and home service companies. Our system connects landing pages, WhatsApp lead capture, quote tracking, booking, job cards, invoice tracking, and maintenance reminders.

We are preparing to launch a Short Video Lead Kit for our clients, but we do not plan to build an internal filming team. We are looking for a reliable video execution partner who can support filming and editing on a white-label or hybrid basis.

The model is simple:

Optimaks will handle:
- monthly content strategy
- hooks and script direction
- WhatsApp CTA
- landing page linkage
- lead tracking and reporting
- client workflow integration

Your team will handle:
- filming or editing
- subtitles
- short-form formatting for TikTok / Reels / Shorts
- delivery of 4–8 videos per month

We are especially interested in content for aircon, repair, and home service businesses, such as before/after shots, technician work process, customer pain points, and WhatsApp booking CTA.

Do you support white-label or hybrid partnership work for monthly recurring clients?

If yes, could you share:
1. partner pricing for 4 videos/month and 8 videos/month
2. onsite shooting availability
3. editing from customer-provided mobile footage
4. turnaround time
5. revision policy
6. whether videos can be delivered without your watermark

Happy to arrange a short call to explore partnership fit.

Best regards,
Paul
Optimaks Pte Ltd
```
